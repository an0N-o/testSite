FEATURES, ADVANTAGE AND BENEFITS
  • Speed - We take half the time of a conventional building
  • GreenBuilding - All materials used are Environment friendly materials.
  • Standardisation - All raw materials used are produced in compliant factories under total quality control systems . Test Certificates are provided with all materials.
  • Earth Quake resistant - LGSF construction is ideal for high wind zone( 200 Kmph) as well as high seismic zone area (Zone V) as the dead load of entire structure system is much lower as compared to RCC.
  • Lighter & Stronger - This entire structure is much lighter (almost 30-35 %) but stronger as compared to RCC building and is a highly earth quake resistant structure system. The entire framing system is manufactured out of Hi-quality Hi tensile Galvanized steel.
  • Longer life & easy to maintain - It provides better habitable environment due to insulated constructions.
  • Building extensions - Being light weight this structure is suitable for all vertical expansions, even on older building structure.
  • Quality Structure - This system used high tensile steel (550Mpa) for structural members with anti-rust zinc coating of 275gsm making it completely weather proof.
  • Customized system - Complete LGSF system is designed as per the client requirements and comes with the design report proving the structural stability. This report can be verified by any competent authority.
  • Environment Friendly - All materials used for construction are factory finished material and are eco friendly in nature.
  • Compliance - This structure complies to all relevant Indian and international codes required for designing the system.
